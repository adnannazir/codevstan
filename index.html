Salary Tax Slabs According to Budget 2026 – 27

    Salary income is under PKR 600,000

    If the annual salary income does not exceed PKR. 600,000 then the rate of the income tax is 0%

    Salary income exceeds PKR 600,000 but is under PKR 1.2 Million

    If the annual salary income exceeds PKR. 600,000 but does not exceed PKR. 1.2 million then the rate of income tax is 1% of the amount exceeding PKR. 600,000.

    Salary income exceeds PKR 1.2 Million but is under PKR 2.2 Million

    If the taxable annual income exceeds PKR. 1.2 million but does not exceed PKR. 2.2 million then the rate of income tax is PKR. 6000 + 11% of the amount exceeding PKR. 1.2 million.

    Salary income exceeds PKR 2.2 Million but is under PKR 3.2 Million

    If the annual salary income exceeds PKR. 2.2 million but does not exceed PKR. 3.2 million then the rate of income tax is PKR 116,000 + 20% of the amount exceeding PKR. 2.2 million

    Salary income exceeds PKR 3.2 Million but is under PKR 4.1 Million

    If the annual salary income exceeds PKR. 4.1 million but does not exceed PKR. 4.1 million then the rate of income tax is PKR. 316,000 + 25% of the amount exceeding PKR. 3.2 million

    Salary income exceeds PKR 4.1 million

    If the annual salary income exceeds PKR. 4.1 million but does not exceed PKR. 5.6 million then the rate of income tax is PKR 541,000 + 29% of the amount exceeding PKR. 4.1 million

    Salary income exceeds PKR 5.6 million

    If the annual salary income exceeds PKR. 5.6 million but does not exceed PKR. 7 million then the rate of income tax is PKR 976,000 + 32% of the amount exceeding PKR. 5.6 million

    Salary income exceeds PKR 7.0 million

    If the annual salary income exceeds PKR. 7.0 million then the rate of income tax is PKR 14,24,000 + 35% of the amount exceeding PKR. 7.0 million
